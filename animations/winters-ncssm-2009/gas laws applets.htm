I am sending this applet from my gmail account in the unlikely event that the filters also grab sent mail as well as received mail. I have modified the gas compression applet to allow for more inputs and more control of the animation by the teacher. However, I do not advise throwing out the older version, because that version had the distinction of calculating the physics for each of the three possible combinations of isobaric, isothermal, and adiabatic processes. In this new version, temperature and pressure will move between given initial and final values regardless of whether the values given actually make any sense. Thus, it is up to the creator of the applet to ensure that the values are realistic.

The relevant inputs are:

pit --- initial pressure
pif --- final pressure
tempi --- initial temperature
tempf --- final temperature
p_l_def -- lower bound of the pressure gauge
p_u_def --- upper bound of the pressure gause
t_l_def --- lower bound of the thermometer
t_u_def --- upper bound of the thermometer
